Rules

1. General conditions:

You must play 30 hours in a randomly selected game from the participants' list within 2 weeks for a new, exciting gaming experience

2. Game selection:

Games are chosen from the Steam Family Sharing library (VR games are not counted). If a participant is not in the family, they must purchase the game on their own. The selection is made by majority vote.

3. Re-roll:

If the previous game has been played for at least 3 hours, the participant may re-roll. In this case, the time resets and 2 new weeks are provided.

4. Penalties:

If the conditions are not met, the participant must purchase a Steam game costing up to 300 rubles for all who completed the challenge.

5. Miscellaneous:

Notification of unforeseen circumstances and the decision to temporarily stop the challenge are made by mutual consent. Time is determined in Steam.

6. Challenge completion:

The challenge is considered complete when the main storyline is finished or 30 hours have been played.

7. Prohibitions:

Actions that artificially increase gaming time (e.g. using cheats, guides, mods, excessive skipping of dialogs, etc.) are prohibited.

8. Selection restrictions:

Each participant selects up to three games. Games proposed by the participant cannot be chosen for them. The same game for different participants is not allowed. Minimum difficulty is "Normal".
